Water Level Recognition by Analyzing the Sound when Pouring Water

  • 論文情報：Publicationsページをご覧ください
    Paper: See the Publications page.
  • 内容：ボトルに水を注ぐ際の音を収録した音声データ
    Content: Audio recordings of water being poured into bottles.
  • 用途：水位推定モデルの学習および評価
    Usage: Training and evaluating water level recognition models.

This dataset contains audio recordings of water being poured into bottles, used for training and evaluating water level recognition models.
